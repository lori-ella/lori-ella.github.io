Introduction

Endmatter Updater

Insert a new page into all of your ebooks at once?

Batch update EPUBS.

Need to change your bio to add your newsletter link but have so many books it seems impossible?

Want to add links to all your new books into your older digital books?

For authors facing the daunting task of updating all of their digital books, the updater batch processes an unlimited number of digital books in EPUB format. All you need is the page to be inserted and watch this application update dozens of books in seconds.
