Requirements

The Endmatter Updater requires the following:
  • Windows or Mac desktop.
  • Digital books in the EPUB format
    Note: To convert files to EPUB format, see: Convert if necessary
  • A zip program that can open EPUB files such as 7-zip
    Note: The new file that will be inserted into all EPUBs during the update should be copied from an updated, verified EPUB to ensure the batch processed EPUBs are properly formed.
